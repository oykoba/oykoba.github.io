M. vaccae

The healing map of Mycobacterium vaccae is an art work in a form of a map based in Kivinokka Helsinki. It takes you on a journey where you explore nature with simple methods and extends your stay in the area. Because Mycobacterium vaccae is also a soil bacterium that releases serotonin and noradrenaline in the brain and you breathe it in while spending time in nature
